Raptor

Welcome to the Raptor server's web-interface that can be used to monitors live builds and view / download results such as Build Monitor and Semmel. Raptor has several components, raptor-cli, raptor-webserver, raptor-backend, raptor-notification-system and the raptor-rest-api.
